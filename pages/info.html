Go To The Squares!

How To Play

Take the last digit in each team's score at the end of each section. If the cross-section of those numbers is your box, you win that section! e.g.: if at the end of the first quarter, the Chiefs are winning 10-7, then the box with the '0' column for the 49ers and '7' row for the 49ers would win the first quarter.


Payouts

There will be payouts for the scores at the end of the First Quarter, First Half, Third Quarter, and Final Score.

That means that if the score is tied at the end of the Fourth Quarter, that person will NOT be paid out. It will go to the winner at the end of the game.
